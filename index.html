Just in Case

Did you ever type unwittingly a lot of text in uppercase, and then you were so annoyed that you would have to type it again?

Did you feel as if you had commited a... capital crime?

Just in case you do it again, here you have a tool to fix it.

Paste your text in the field below, choose what you want to do, and press Convert.

  • All uppercase
  • All lowercase
  • alternating
  • Reverse order
  • Switch the cases
  • camelCase
  • PascalCase
  • snake_case
  • Clear
  • Copy to clipboard
